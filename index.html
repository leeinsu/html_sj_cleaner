Commercial Cleaning

The process of cleaning and maintaining business properties such as offices, factories, hospitals, and retail stores is known as commercial cleaning. Vacuuming, mopping, dusting, window cleaning, and trash collection are all part of the job.

Office Cleaning

Office cleaning refers to the process of keeping office buildings or workspaces clean and maintained. It involves regular tasks such as sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, dusting, polishing, and sanitizing. The frequency of office cleaning varies depending on the needs of the organization, which could be daily, or weekly.
